## Core Concepts

The cognitive shadow theory establishes fundamental limits on what can be observed, 
measured, and reconstructed regarding subjective experience from physical signatures.

### Theorem 1′ (On the cognitive shadow)
For any agent and any formal system, there exists an experience that cannot be fully 
formalised, transferred, or exhaustively measured.

### Theorem 10 (On the principle of signature observability)
The signature mapping is non‑injective, reconstruction is bounded, and AUC ∈ (0.5, 1).
